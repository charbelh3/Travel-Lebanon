Travel Lebanon: Privacy Policy

This page is intended to inform users about our policies regarding the collection and use of personal information.

If you choose to use Travel Lebanon, you are agreeing to the collection and use of information as outlined in this policy. The personal information we collect is used to provide and enhance the service. We will not use or share your information with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy.

How do we handle user preferences?

The user's preferred theme and saved favorite places are only stored on the user's device. We do not collect or store any user preference-related data on our systems. This data can be deleted from the user's device by clearing the app's data.

What services do we need access to?

  • Internet access is required to connect to our servers and load information about different cities and tourist sites from our database.
  • This app may, with the user's permission, use the user's location to enhance the service by displaying nearby tourist sites and filtering results based on the user's proximity.

Analytics

We utilize Firebase Analytics and/or Google Analytics to collect information about how our app is used. These services help us understand the number of users, their interactions with the app, and may include aggregated data such as users' cities and device types. This information is crucial for making informed decisions to improve the user experience. The data collected is anonymous and does not personally identify you. By using our app, you consent to the processing of data by Firebase Analytics and/or Google Analytics in the manner and for the purposes set out above.

External Links

Our app may contain links to external sites, such as Google Forms for submitting suggestions and a "Buy Me a Coffee" website if you choose to donate. Please note that we do not personally operate these sites. Clicking on these links will take you to third-party websites with their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these external sites before providing any personal information.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or inquiries regarding this privacy policy or our app, please feel free to contact us at travelebanon.lb@gmail.com.
